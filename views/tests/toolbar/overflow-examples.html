Toolbar Test: Overflow Examples

The following methods of toolbar item overflow are described in this JIRA Ticket:

  • 1 Button: all buttons may be shown
  • 2 Buttons: all buttons may be shown
  • 3 Buttons: all buttons may be shown
  • More than 3 Buttons: "More Actions" button becomes present, Button 3 and above spill into overflow menu.
